* Project Title
---------------
Kyklos

* Project Description
---------------------
For Ludum Dare Game Jam 47, under the theme of "Stuck in a loop", this game was inspired by the movie Edge of Tomorrow and used the concept of dying and returning with newly gained knowledge to progress through.

* Description
-------------
In four days a catastrophe will come and wipe out your village.  Play as a knight, fight bosses, interact with NPCs, and solve the puzzle of how to save your town.  The village depends on you, but don't worry.  If you fail, you can turn back time and reset the days to 0.  Use what you learned to save the village once and for all.
